\section{Conclusion}

We presented CMatrix, a multi-agent orchestration framework that leverages a composite reasoning suite to achieve resilient and efficient autonomous red teaming. By integrating Tree of Thoughts, ReWOO, and executable reflection, CMatrix achieves a 97.4\% success rate across a wide range of security tasks while reducing token overhead by 2.42$\times$. Furthermore, the integration of a risk-aware HITL safety gate provides a verifiable governance model for autonomous security operations. Our findings demonstrate that the deliberate composition of advanced reasoning patterns is a necessary foundation for the next generation of trustworthy and scalable autonomous security agents.
